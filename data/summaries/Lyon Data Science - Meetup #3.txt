Marc Fackin introduces Trai au Tuba, a place for experimentation and innovation in developing new services for the city that use or generate digital data. They focus on accompanying startups and developing services with the end user, the citizen, in mind. They are currently experimenting with a client platform service for GRDF and have a variety of projects in the works, including one focused on energy data.
