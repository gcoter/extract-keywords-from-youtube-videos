Data analysts from Inato and Front discuss the collaboration between product management and data analysis to promote a self-service platform and increase data culture within the company. They provide examples of how product data analysts intervene in each phase of product conception, using Inato as an example. The article discusses the importance of tracking user interactions with a product and the need for clear rules and guidelines for tracking. The team is rethinking their data visualization and exploration tools and trying to allocate more time for data analysts to work on high-value projects. They recommend having a dedicated data analyst for product development to develop specific skills and work closely with the product team.
